About me

Hi, I'm Evan! I'm an NDSEG Fellow and a Robotics Ph.D. Student at the Oregon State University Robotic Decision Making Laboratory where I'm advised by Geoff Hollinger. The goal of my research is to create collaborative robots that can be safely and successfully deployed into the real world.

If I'm not in the lab working on my latest crazy idea, you can find me outdoors, training to hit a new PR in the gym, or playing video games with my friends.

Please feel free to contact me with any questions or regarding potential collaborations!

Problems that I'm thinking about

  • Robot Collaboration

    Can we design robots that collaborate with each other to accomplish complex tasks in dynamic environments?

  • Safety

    How can we best incorporate safety verification into robotic systems so that they operate in a safe, sustainable way?

  • Human-Robot Interaction

    What are the best ways to ensure that the robots that we design are usable and easily understood by humans?

  • Field Robotics

    How do we overcome the challenges associated with deploying our systems into the real world?
